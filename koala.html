KOALAS

The koala is an arboreal herbivorous marsupial native to Australia it is the only representative of the family Phascolarctidae and its closest living relatives are the wombats.

Koalas typically inhabit open eucalypt woodlands, and the leaves of these trees make up most of their diet.

You will find the koalas next to the giraffe enclosure.
